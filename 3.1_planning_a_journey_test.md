## 2
Is it mandatory to use a safety seat for children?

A) Yes, for children under 3.

B) Yes, for children under 135 cm.

C) No.
NB. Answer is B

## 3
You have a B-class licence. Which combination of passenger car and trailer are you allowed to drive?

A) Vehicle with the complete weight of at most 3500 kg and trailer with the complete weight of 2000 kg

B) Vehicle with the complete weight of at most 3500 kg and trailer with the complete weight of 750 kg

C) Vehicle with the complete weight of at most 3900 kg and trailer with the complete weight of 750 kg
NB. Answer is B

## 4
How do you have to pay attention to the centre of mass when loading the car?

A) Heavy things should be placed in the roof box.

B) Heavy things should be placed in the backseat.

C) Heavy things should be placed in the boot.
N.B Answer is C

## 6
Before you attach the trailer to a passenger car, you have to check whether it's legal. Where do you check it?

A) From the model signs on the car and trailer.

B) From the manuals of the car and trailer.

C) From the registration of the car and trailer or using Traficom website service.

B) When it's over 1 metre.

C) When it's over 2 metres.

## 9
Can the driver type a new address in the navigator while driving?

A) Yes, provided this involves no danger.

B) No, under no circumstances.

Answer is B, NB. Road Traffic Act section 98.

## 10
You have to check a lot of things in the car before the drive. Which of the following is most important?

A) The amount of the coolant.

B) Tyre pressures.

C) The amount of oil.

NB. Answer is B. Especially when going on a longer journey, it's a good idea to check all of these. Additionally, the functionality of the lights and the correct loading of the car should be ensured.
