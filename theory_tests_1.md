### 1 What should be taken into caution upon taking over a cyclist?
 
Correct answer: There must be enough space in between so that the air flow does not grab the cyclist.
Your answer: It is necessary to ensure that the cyclist is riding the road's shoulder.

### 2 What is the correct way to act at an accident scene when no helpers are there yet?
 
Correct answer: Warn other people, call for help, rescue the injured.
Your answer: Rescue the injured, warn other people, call for help.
Explanation: In traffic accidents, there is a general duty to help. Assist the victims, but do not stay as a bystander 
if help is already on the scene.

### 6 What is regulated regarding turning signal?
 
Correct answer: A turning signal must always be given when turning in an intersection.
Your answer: A turning signal is not mandatory, if the vehicle does not have an indicating system.
Explanation: Using your turn signal improves traffic safety and flow. Make sure to indicate your intentions well in advance.

### 7 You are driving during winter and it is snowing. The snow is melting on the windshield, freezing the windshield wipers. What will this cause? How can you fix it?

Your answer: The weather conditions cannot be changed. Even though visibility is poor, I will keep driving and try to find a spot on the windshield that is clear enough to see through.

## Risks

### 10 You are driving on a road and there is an oncoming snow plow truck. What should be noted about this situation?
 
Correct answer: Plow truck can throw a lot of snow in the air and the visibility can thus be very low behind them.
Your answer: Plow trucks always have the right of way.
Explanation: 

### 11 How does fog impact your driving?
 
Correct answer: In fog, visibility is worse. The grip may also be worse if the road surface is wet. Slow down just in case.
Your answer: In fog only grip is impacted. Be prepared for it.
Explanation:

### 12 You see a child on the edge of the road. How can you know how the situation will evolve? What must you do?
 
Correct answer: You cannot always predict a child's way of thinking. The child may come into the road, so I drive further from the verge. I monitor the situation and get ready to do an emergency stop.
Your answer: You cannot always predict a child's way of thinking. Nevertheless, I trust that nothing bad will happen.
Explanation: 

### 13 You are driving on a road and there is an oncoming snow plow truck. What should be noted about this situation?
 
Correct answer: Plow truck can throw a lot of snow in the air and the visibility can thus be very low behind them.
Your answer: It is a good idea to turn on high-beam lights to get the best possible visibility.
Explanation:
